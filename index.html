About Our Store

Our Story

To provide the finest fragrances that create lasting memories for our customers across Kenya and beyond.

Welcome to Udgoon Collection, Nairobi's premier destination for authentic Arabian and international perfumes.

We envision a world where everyone can access luxury fragrances at affordable prices, making elegance accessible to all.

Founded in 2026, Udgoon Collection has quickly become Kenya's trusted source for original Lattafa perfumes and other premium fragrance brands.

"Every bottle tells a story, every scent creates a memory. This is the philosophy behind Udgoon Collection."

We guarantee 100% authentic products or your money back!

We offer same-day delivery within Nairobi and 24-48 hour delivery to all major towns across Kenya.

All prices are inclusive of VAT. Free delivery on orders above KSh 5,000.

Quality You Can Trust - Every Bottle, Every Time

Beware of counterfeit products. Always buy from authorized dealers like Udgoon Collection.

Our customer service team is available 24/7 to answer your questions and help you choose the perfect fragrance.
